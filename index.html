COVID-19 Trends

COVID-19 first appeared in China in late 2019 and rapdily spread across the globe. In March 2020, COVID was declared a pandemic by the WHO, as it led to millions of deaths and overwhelemed healthcare systems. Lockdowns, school cloosures, and economic shutodnws had lasting social and financial impacts. As the world entered crisis mode, vaccination rollouts varied siginficantly across countries, with disparities in total vaccinations, coverage per hundred people, etc. These differences highlight both the urgency of pandemic response and the inequality in access to healthcare resources. By anaylzing this dataset, we can better understand how vaccine distribution shaped the course of the pandemic, and which countries took quick action.
